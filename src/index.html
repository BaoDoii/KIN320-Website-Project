Our Mission

We Watch.
We Analyze.
We Verdict.

Reel Sport is a sport film criticism magazine built on the belief that athletic cinema deserves rigorous, uncompromising analysis.

Our critics bring deep knowledge of both sport and film to every review — going beyond plot to examine theme, technique, cultural context, and the role sport plays in how we tell stories.
